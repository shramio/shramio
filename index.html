About Shramio

Shramio is a helpline-based labour supply company that connects daily wage labourers with construction companies in Tier-2 city. We focus on simplicity, reliability, and trust to ensure steady work and smooth project execution.

The Problem

  • Daily labourers struggle to find consistent work
  • Construction companies face daily labour shortages
  • Existing solutions depend on apps or middlemen

Our Solution

Shramio solves this problem through a simple phone-call based helpline system. Labourers call us to register availability, and contractors call us to request labour. We quickly match the right workers to the right site.
